Welcome to the official Artistech information/shopping website. In here you will learn About artisTECH and how the idea originated, how artisTECH works, and you will have the chance to buy it too.

Here is the how artisTECH originated

artisTECH originated when I thought of the question, "what type of technological innovation can make fine art or drawing easier. So I thought of some problems that people have with drawing and I noticed that some people feel they aren't able to accurately draw things that come to their mind. THis is obviously because drawing and art takes a lot of skill. So that was my problem and my solution was pretty simple. I thought of the Idea to create a machine that can read your mind and draw out things that you thought of ACCURATELY.

Here is how the artisTECH is created

artisTECH is very simple it reads your mind and takes the data of what you are thinking of to draw and draws it for you. The artisTECH comes in 2 parts one is a tiny computer chip that you stick to your forehead and the other is a tablet. The process is that when you think of something to draw the chip will pick it up and send the data to the tablet and the tablet will use the data to map out coordinates and then eventually come up with the final drawing that you were thinking of.
